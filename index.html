About Me

Im an all around web developer with a passion for Python application development. I believe testing is important on all levels and prefer to test from a behavioral standpoint. I have a diverse set of skills ranging from working with front end libraries such as React and VueJS to full fledged MVCs such as Django and Ruby on Rails. I often blend these contrasting passions as I solve issues I come across in my outdoor pursuits with sensible technological solutions, making it safer and easier to access outdoor resources.
